    Image-Text Alignment

    A core requirement of Assignment 1

    The central requirement:

    Your GenAI image must visually reflect your written narrative, and your written narrative must reference and describe your GenAI image and the prompt you used to generate it. These two elements must speak to each other — they are one unified submission, not two separate pieces.

    🖼 Image → Text

    • Your image reflects your academic literacy journey theme
    • Your image connects to the ideas in your narrative
    • Your image is intentionally chosen — not random or decorative

    ✍️ Text → Image

    • Your narrative includes your exact GenAI prompt
    • Your prompt uses both English and your home language (translanguaging)
    • Your narrative describes what the image depicts and why you chose it
    Common mistakes to avoid:
    • Using a decorative AI image with no connection to your narrative theme
    • Not including your exact GenAI prompt in the Image & Prompt section
    • Writing your prompt in English only — translanguaging is required
    • Describing the image superficially without connecting it to your learning journey
    For the Lecturer — Marking Criterion 2 (5 marks):

    Evaluate: (1) Is the image clearly AI-generated? (2) Is the prompt intentional and creative? (3) Does the image visually reflect the narrative themes? (4) Is translanguaging (English + L1) present in the prompt? (5) Does the student's text adequately reference and connect to the image?
